About Me

Hi There! My name's Tom Roff, a programmer and 3D artist from Houston, Texas. I love learning new tools and tricks and applying them to team-based project environments. I'm creative but always open to feedback on how I can improve my work, and always eager to pick up new work on something that could contribute to a project.


  • Born in Houston, TX
  • Attending Davidson College, set to graduate in May 2025.
  • Enjoys singing in the car, hiking, and baking
  • ...Has about a 60% success rate in baking
  • Passionate about making virtual experiences since age 5
  • Loves my cat, Mufasa

Projects



Vanguard Division

An independently developed multiplayer shooter with tower-defence gameplay against endless waves of robots.


Skills Used:

  • 250+ models created in Blender, including basic rigging and UV map creation
  • 150+ of these models given realistic textures in Substance Painter
  • Hundreds of gameplay mechanics and actors created using Unreal Engine 4's Blueprint system
  • Custom and Dynamic Materials made using Unreal's Material System
  • Dozens of VFX created using the Niagara Particle System

Habitat for Humanity Architectural Visualization

High-fidelity 3D renders used to demonstrate proposed renovations to a Habitat for Humanity ReStore location.


Skills Used:

  • Collaborated with store executives to understand their needs and goals for the renovations
  • 100+ models measured to real-life scale and faithfully recreated in Blender
  • Recreated realistic outdoor and indoor lighting to better convey lighting solutions to the Habitat for Humanity team
  • Received feedback and made changes to model according to team needs in an evolving process
